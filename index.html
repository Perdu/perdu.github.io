Célestin Matte

DevOps System Administrator



I'm a freelance devops system administrator. I deploy and migrate infrastructures for small companies and startups in an automated, documented, and trackable way using Ansible, git and Docker. I ensure resiliency of systems through monitoring, redundancy and fast automatic redeployment.

I have an engineering degree and a PhD, which give me both autonomy and efficiency. During my thesis, I developed and set up a demo Wi-Fi tracking system that I deployed in several places, notably in a 11-month exhibition in the Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie in Paris, with strong availability and self-sufficiency constraints. I've also been maintaining my own personal infrastructure of 4 servers for emails, web and other services for 8 years, which brings me a long-term experience of system administration and incidents management.
