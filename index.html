Célestin Matte

Privacy researcher, devops engineer, digital artist


You have a project that requires building from code development to long-term system administration and maintenance? I'm your guy!
I have a long experience and passion of independently building projects from scratch and maintaining them over the years. For instance, I currently run as personal projects:

  • an online video game,
  • a service for collecting and displaying statistics of a video game,
  • my personal servers, including three web servers, mail, OpenVPN and other services,
  • bots for Jabber/XMPP chatrooms.

Research-wise, I worked on Wi-Fi and web tracking and collaborated with lawyers. In my thesis, I found ways to circumvent MAC address randomization, which constitute privacy issues as it simplifies tracking of Wi-Fi-enabled devices (e.g. smartphones). A demo Wi-Fi tracking system using a network of Raspberry Pis I developed was installed in various exhibitions, among which the Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie in Paris for 11 months. I then worked on web tracking, and found legal issues in both cookie banners using IAB Europe's Transparency & Consent Framework, and definition of purposes in this framework.

I'm also passionate about dance, and created shows using a Kinect-based system I developed.

Do contact me with your new, ambitious, challenging project!
